Women's Rights & Indian Laws

The Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005

This law provides protection to women from domestic abuse and violence within their households.

The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act, 2013

This act ensures a safe working environment for women by addressing sexual harassment cases.

The Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961

It prohibits the giving and taking of dowry and imposes strict punishments on those involved.

The Maternity Benefit Act, 1961

It provides maternity leave and protection to working women before and after childbirth.

The Hindu Succession Act, 1956 (Amended in 2005)

This amendment grants equal inheritance rights to daughters in their family property.

The Medical Termination of Pregnancy Act, 1971

This act allows women to legally terminate pregnancies under specific conditions.
